Abstract Submission

The deadline for submitting the abstract is June 23, 2024.

To submit your abstract please proceed as follows:
  • Download the LaTeX-template TemplateAbstract.tex and use this file to write your abstract.
  • Please do not insert additional user-defined commands or load additional packages.
  • Make sure that your abstract compiles with pdflatex without errors.
  • Before upload, please rename your file as Surname-Firstname.tex.
