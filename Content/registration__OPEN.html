Registration

To register for the conference, please fill and submit the registration form and pay the conference fee.

Conference fee

The standard conference fee is € 200 (this includes: welcome coffee, the admission to all scientific sessions, conference packages, room setup and use of audio/visual equipment, evening event in a typical Viennese Restaurant). The reduced conference fee is € 130 (this includes: welcome coffee, the admission to all scientific sessions, conference packages, room setup and use of audio/visual equipment).

The conference fee should be paid by bank transfer (domestic and foreign fees charged to payer) after you have received an invoice.
