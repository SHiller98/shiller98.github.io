Guided tour

On Wednesday 10.7.2024 there will be a guided tour through the inner city. It starts Wednesday 10.7.2024, 18'00 and takes approximately 2 hours. We will meet at the Vienna state opera building (Herbert-von-Karajan Platz, see this map).

Everybody is welcome to participate. Please tell us if you want to participate on Monday 8.7 upon in-person registration. If you arrive only later and want to participate, please send an email to conference email address ( ota2024 (at) tuwien.ac.at ) until latest Monday 8.7, 10'00.
